Cookie Policy

Effective Date: January 30, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. In this policy, "we," "us," and "our" refer to Nithichote Chongrungruang, operating as AI Automation Consultant, including any authorized agents, contractors, team members, or service providers acting on our behalf. This policy provides you with clear and comprehensive information about the cookies we use, their purpose, and your choices regarding their use.

Quick Summary

We use minimal cookies to ensure our website functions properly and to understand how visitors use our site. We respect your privacy and provide you with full control over cookie usage. Essential cookies are required for basic functionality, while analytics cookies help us improve your experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They serve various purposes:

  • Session Management: Keeping you logged in, remembering your preferences
  • Personalization: Storing your settings and preferences
  • Tracking: Understanding how you use our website to improve it

1.1 Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use:

  • Local Storage: Similar to cookies but can store more data
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage that expires when you close your browser
  • Pixel Tags: Tiny images that help us understand email engagement

2. Legal Basis for Using Cookies

2.1 GDPR Compliance (EU/EEA Visitors)

We process cookies based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: For essential cookies required for website functionality
  • Consent: For analytics and functional cookies that enhance your experience

2.2 CCPA Compliance (California Residents)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect through cookies and to opt-out of any sale of personal information (we do not sell your data).

5. How We Use Cookie Data

We use information collected through cookies to:

  • Ensure Functionality: Maintain your session and form data
  • Improve Performance: Identify and fix technical issues
  • Enhance User Experience: Understand how visitors navigate our site
  • Facilitate Bookings: Enable smooth appointment scheduling
  • Analyze Traffic: Understand visitor patterns and optimize content
  • Prevent Fraud: Detect and prevent malicious activity

5.1 Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell cookie data to third parties
  • Share cookie data for advertising purposes
  • Use cookies for behavioral targeting
  • Create user profiles for marketing

6. Your Cookie Choices and Rights

6.1 Consent Management

When you first visit our website, we will ask for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences by category
  • Change your preferences at any time

6.2 Browser Controls

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

6.3 Opt-Out Links

You can opt-out of specific services:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Cal.com: Manage through their privacy settings when booking

6.4 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality:

  • Essential Cookies: Website may not function properly
  • Functional Cookies: Reduced personalization and convenience
  • Analytics Cookies: We won't be able to improve your experience

7. Cookie-Free Alternatives

We respect your choice to avoid cookies. Alternatives include:

  • Direct Contact: Email hi@nithichote.com to schedule consultations
  • Private Browsing: Use incognito/private mode for temporary sessions
  • Regular Clearing: Clear cookies after each visit
  • Essential Only: Allow only strictly necessary cookies

8. Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect your privacy preferences. Our response to Do Not Track signals:

  • We honor DNT signals by not loading analytics cookies
  • Essential cookies remain active for functionality
  • No behavioral tracking is performed regardless of DNT
